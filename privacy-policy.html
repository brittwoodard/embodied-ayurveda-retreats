Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and understand how important it is to feel secure when sharing your personal information online. We collect only the necessary information to enhance your experience with us—whether you're visiting our site, signing up for a retreat, or simply exploring what we offer.

What information do we collect?

When you interact with us—whether through registering, purchasing, or simply browsing—we may ask for details like your name, email address, or phone number. This helps us provide personalized service and tailor our offerings to what you're most interested in.

When do we collect it?

We gather information when you fill out a form, sign up for a course, or interact with us in other ways on our site.

How do we use your information?

We use your information to:

  • Personalize your experience, ensuring you're seeing content and offers that resonate with you
  • Improve customer service and address any inquiries you may have
  • Follow up with you after conversations or to provide updates on our offerings

Your security

While we don’t ask for credit card numbers and focus primarily on providing helpful content and resources, we do scan for malware regularly to keep your browsing experience safe.

Cookies

Like most websites, we use cookies to understand your preferences and make your experience smoother each time you visit. You can choose to adjust your browser settings to limit cookies if you prefer, but some features of our site may not work as efficiently without them.

Third-party sharing

We don’t sell or trade your personal information. Trusted partners who help us operate our website or assist with business operations may have access to it, but only if they agree to keep it confidential.

Third-party links

We don't include third-party products or services on our site.

Google and tracking

While we haven’t enabled Google AdSense, we may in the future. We respect “Do Not Track” signals and avoid third-party behavioral tracking.

COPPA

We don’t target or market to children under 13, in line with COPPA guidelines.

Fair Information Practices

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify you via email within seven business days. You have the right to pursue legal action if we don’t adhere to privacy laws.

CAN-SPAM Act

We collect your email to provide updates or respond to inquiries. We respect your right to opt out at any time. Simply click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of our emails or contact us directly at connect@embodiedayurvedaretreats.com, and we’ll promptly remove you from all correspondence.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us. We're here to ensure your information is handled with care and respect.
